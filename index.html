Welcome to the new OneeChan page!

OneeChan has recently switched from userstyle to a userscript. The benefits of this are near endless! Now you no longer have to reinstall to change your options, you can customize OneeChan's colors to your liking right from the page, and there's no cost to performance.

How do I get started?

You just need Firefox with Scriptish or Google Chrome / Chromium with the --enable-easy-off-store-extension-install target flag. Then you need to install 4chan X and in its settings enable "Persistent QR." Now, make sure the old OneeChan userstyle is disabled in Stylish if you have it. Click install above, and report any issues you find.
